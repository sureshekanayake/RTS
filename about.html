Welcome to Rameca Technology Solutions

Rameca Technology Solutions is a technology firm located in Kohuwala Sri Lanka. We are a start- up company offering web and internet services to small businesses and institutions in Sri Lanka. We offer services in Digital Marketing, website design, Software development, hosting, maintenance, web analysis, and artwork designing.

We have ventured into the ICT solution industry to offer superior and unique services to small businesses and institutions. Our experience and expertise in web sales and e-commerce gives us a backing to provide solutions that are currently lacking in the market.

The high costs of the projects and the focus on bigger companies and institutions could be the contributing factor as to why this market remains untapped. We have a system that will reduce the project costs dramatically giving us the opportunity to offer quality services at reduced costs.

Our Vision

Our Vision at Rameca Technology Solution is to provide a truly innovative and creative technological solution to a wide range of business.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide accessible, and streamlined web and Internet services to businesses and institutions. Using our system, we will offer various types of web solutions that will be tailored to serve the changing needs of our clients.

Company Ownership

Rameca Technology Solutions (Pvt) Ltd is one of the subsidiary companies of Rameca Group of companies has its offices and facilities in Kohuwala, Sri Lanka. The company is co-owned by Mr. Gotabaya Abayagunawardena, the chairman and joint managing director and Mrs. Jayani Silva, joint managing director.
